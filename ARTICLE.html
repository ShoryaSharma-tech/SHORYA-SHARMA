CHANDRAYAAN 3

Chandrayaan-3 Successfully Lands on Moon

India has created history by successfully landing Chandrayaan-3 on the surface of the moon. This makes India the fourth country in the world to achieve this milestone and the first to land near the moon's south pole.

The mission was launched by ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) and aims to study the moon's surface, soil, and resources. Scientists believe this will help in future space research and missions.

Prime Minister and leaders from all over the world congratulated the ISRO team for this great achievement.
